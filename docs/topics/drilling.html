ThinkGeoEnergy · added 2026-07-02 · relevance: medium

Two companies, OMV and Energie Steiermark, have partnered to develop a geothermal heating project in Graz, Austria. Geothermal heating uses hot rock underground to provide warmth to buildings. They plan to start exploration drilling—the process of making holes deep into the earth to find and assess hot rock resources—by the end of 2026.
