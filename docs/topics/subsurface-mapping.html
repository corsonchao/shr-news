▲ Enhanced Geothermal Systems Demonstration Projects - Department of Energy (.gov)

The Department of Energy is funding demonstration projects for Enhanced Geothermal Systems (EGS), which are engineered reservoirs created by fracturing hot rock deep underground and circulating fluid through it to extract heat for energy. These projects are testing whether EGS technology can reliably produce electricity from superhot rock formations where natural geothermal conditions don't exist.
